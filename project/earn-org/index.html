EARN

OVERVIEW

EARN.org is a non-profit organization that help low income families to learn how to save.

PROBLEM

EARN.org is receiving too many customer support tickets.

SOLUTION

By re-designing EARN's help center we were able to empower EARN's users to answer their questions without having to submit a ticket.

CODING INITIAL PROTOTYPE

INITIAL PROTOTYPE

TESTING

6 User
30 Minutes

During usability testing we asked our users to take a look at some exisiting help center pages and walk us through their process be if using that page to find an answer to a question.

After that we had our users test our initial prototype on a moblie device to see what areas we needed to change to make our site the best it could be.

We then took all of our notes from the usability testing and did some infinity mapping to se what common themes there were across the users experiences.

All of this feedback help to form our final interations.

FINAL ITERATIONS

Using what we learned from our usability testing we created high fidelity mock-ups and conducted more testing of the updated design.

CONCLUSION

This was a great project to work on. I would have wanted to do more research into other help center hosting options that may be more cost effective than the current program and allow for more customization. Along with running another round of usability testing to ensure our solutions really meet the users needs.
