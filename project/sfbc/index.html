OVERVIEW

The SFBC is a non-profit organization that is committed to making the city of San Francisco safer for all cyclists.

PROBLEM

As a concept project SFBC needed a strategy and design for digital tools that would engage their members more frequently and give members a sense of active contribution to the SFBC’s larger goals.

SOLUTION

A mobile app that allows San Francisco Bicycle Coalition (SFBC) members to easily report and track issues

THE RESEARCH

SURVEYS

We found out that most cyclists have heard about SFBC, but over 75% of cyclists said they are not members.

86%

said personal safety is their main issue (fear of being hit by uber, lyft or taxi)

82%

said road conditions are their main issue

INTERVIEWS

We interviewed some frequent cyclists and came away with similar trends regarding personal safety, road conditions, and lack of safe parking.

“Increasing enforcement to reduce risk of theft, add more bike lanes, better car driver awareness”
